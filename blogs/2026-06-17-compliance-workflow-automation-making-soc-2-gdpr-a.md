This piece explains how workflow automation makes compliance frameworks like SOC 2, GDPR, and ISO 27001 auditable by design by generating evidence as a byproduct of automated processes rather than through manual documentation. Three design principles make workflows audit-ready: immutable, cryptographically-linked logging; evidence collected at control execution; and framework-specific automation for access reviews, breach notification, and risk monitoring.
